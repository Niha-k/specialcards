Order and Returns

Order Policy

Thank you for shopping with us! Below are the details of our ordering process:

  • Order Confirmation After you place an order, you will receive an order confirmation email with your order number and details.
  • Payment We accept various forms of payment including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure methods.
  • Shipping Orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary depending on your location.
  • Delivery We offer various delivery options including standard and expedited shipping.

Returns Policy

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of receiving your order. Please review our return process below:

  • Return Eligibility Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resellable condition to qualify for a return.
  • Return Process To initiate a return, contact us with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return authorization and instructions.
  • Refunds Once your return is received and inspected, a refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Exchanges If you'd like to exchange an item, we can assist you with the exchange process based on availability.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons, or they may be marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase. These include:

  • Personalized items
  • Open software or digital downloads
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as non-returnable during checkout
